greatestdepth

Occurrence

compulsory, single

Attributes

—

Description

Within <greatestdepth> the greatest depth in metres (given as a real number) reached during a specific dive is put into brackets (not the maximum reacheable depth at this dive spot — see <maximumdepth>!). Even if the profile data are stored inside <samples>, <greatestdepth> must be given.

Parent Elements

<informationafterdive>

Child Elements

—

See Also

<dive>, <informationbeforedive>

Examples

<!-- greatest depth reached during this dive -->
<!-- (the maximum reacheable depth at this site could be 60 m for example -->
<!-- and would be put into brackets within <maximumdepth> inside <sitedata>) -->
<greatestdepth>48.5 </greatestdepth>
