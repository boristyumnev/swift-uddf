tankpressureend

Occurrence

optional, single

Attributes

—

Description

<tankpressureend> puts the fill pressure of the tank at the end of the dive, given in Pascal as a real number, into brackets.

Contrary to <tankpressurebegin> which must be given, <tankpressureend>is optional — as far as <tankvolume> is given to perform a breathing gas consumption calculation.

Parent Elements

<tankdata>

Child Elements

—

See Also

<tankdata>, <tankpressurebegin>, <tankpressurereserve>, <tankvolume>

Examples

(see also <tankdata>) 

<!-- tank pressure at end of dive 104 bar -->
<tankpressureend>10400000.0</tankpressureend>
