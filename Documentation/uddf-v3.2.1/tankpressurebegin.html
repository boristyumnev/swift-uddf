tankpressurebegin

Occurrence

compulsory, single

Attributes

—

Description

<tankpressurebegin> puts the fill pressure of the tank at the beginning of the dive, given in Pascal as a real number, into brackets.

Contrary to <tankvolume>, or <tankpressureend>, which can be given, <tankpressurebegin> must be given inside the <tankdata> section, because without this information no breathing gas consumption calculation can be performed.

Parent Elements

<tankdata>

Child Elements

—

See Also

<tankdata>, <tankpressureend>, <tankpressurereserve>, <tankvolume>

Examples

(see also <tankdata>) 

<!-- tank pressure at beginning of dive 210 bar -->
<tankpressurebegin>21000000.0</tankpressurebegin>
