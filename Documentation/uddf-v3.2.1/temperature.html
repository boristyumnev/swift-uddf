temperature

Occurrence

optional, single

Attributes

—

Description

<temperature> indicates the water temperature during the dive, given in Kelvin as a real number.

The water temperature is considered to be constant until a next <temperature> statement alters it.

Parent Elements

<waypoint>

Child Elements

—

See Also

<samples>, <waypoint>

Examples

(see also <samples>) 

<!-- water temperature 20 °C -->
<temperature>293.15</temperature>
